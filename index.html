Outsource Production

Why Use McColl Cabinets
to Outsource Production?

Why Use McColl Cabinets to Outsource Production? McColl Cabinets have over 25 years of industry experience, specialising in high-quality custom cabinetry. We have an extremely automated workshop that includes CNC capabilities and  modern corner rounding edge bander, allowing us to efficiently manufacture superior cabinetry that is ready to install. This includes all brands and products that are currently available in melamine, laminate, vinyl and 2pack painted finishes.

So, how does outsourcing to McColl Cabinets work? It’s very simple. All you need to do is supply us with a sketched layout and dimensions so that we can provide a quote (which you will receive within 48 hours – minimum order quantity of $1,000). You’ll then be able to select from our list of available colours and materials and we’ll complete working drawings for your final approval. Once approved, we’ll manufacture, assemble and deliver your cabinets to the job site – all you need to do is meet us there and complete the installation.

About Us

Established in 1993, we have over 25 years of experience in the industry and service Brisbane, Ipswich and surround-ing suburbs.
